Slide 4: Geographic Hedging - Portfolio Diversification Benefits

*Illustrative*

Diversification = Resilience:
  • Mature vs Growth Markets: Nordics provide liquidity and regulatory stability; Greece/Eastern Europe offer higher margins with less competition
  • First-Mover Advantage: Early entry in emerging markets (Greece, Poland) captures premium pricing before saturation

  • Liquidity Benefits: Deep Nordic markets enable large positions; emerging markets provide outsized returns on smaller deployments
